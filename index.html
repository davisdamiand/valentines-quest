The Longing of My Soul

Dear Reader, are you ready to embark on an exciting quest to become one’s Valentine?

You must follow the instructions to the letter. Three artifacts must be prepared and kept on hand by Saturday the 14th at exactly 11:00. Each artifact is as important as the next, for only with them shall you be granted safe passage along the Road of Amor Aeternus by the Ferry of Amor.

The Lonely One requires: a drawing of pure beauty, a flower that has lost the will to live, and a stone unlike any other, engraved with the cries of one’s heart. The Lonely One will request these artifacts upon your arrival.

An honorable exchange shall then take place, for the law decrees: Humankind cannot gain anything without first giving something in return. To obtain, something of equal value must be lost. This is alchemy’s First Law of Equivalent Exchange.

Dear Reader, are you prepared for the journey upon the Road of Amor Aeternus?
